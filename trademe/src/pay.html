Pay a fine

You can pay your vehicle, parking, bus lane, transit lane, harbourmaster and public transport fines online, in person or by post.

Fine not showing online

If your fine details are not showing online, it may be because the fine was issued within the last 24 hours. Please try again later today or tomorrow. If the problem persists, please contact us.

Pay online with credit or debit card

Pay with Visa or Mastercard credit or debit card. No fees apply.


Pay online with Account2Account

Pay online with our secure third party direct bank transfer Account2Account. No fees apply. Available for these banks: ANZ, ASB, BNZ, KiwiBank, TSB, Westpac. Read how it works


Pay online in four installments with Zip

Pay in four equal installments over six weeks using Zip. No interest or fees.

Once you have entered your infringement details, select Zip as your payment method. Then you can create a Zip account if you don't already have one, or log in to your existing Zip account. Payments are made with the credit or debit card added to your Zip account.

The first payment is processed immediately, and the remaining payments are made fortnightly at 2 weeks, 4 weeks and 6 weeks after the first payment is made.

Read the instalment plan terms & conditions.

Pay in person

When you pay in person, please remember to take your infringement notice with you, as it has the payment barcode printed on it.

Vehicle, parking, transit lane and bus lane fines can be paid in person at:

  • New Zealand Postshops anywhere in New Zealand.
  • BNZ Auckland Branches.

Public transport and harbourmaster fines can be paid in person at:

  • New Zealand Postshops anywhere in New Zealand.

Pay by post

Throughout 2021 all major New Zealand banks will stop issuing and accepting cheques as a method of payment.

As a result, Auckland Transport no longer accept cheques.

See our other ways to pay or contact your bank for help.
